ABOUT US.

We are a group of students Amin Sharvari, Cipparrone Mya, Koo Minjung (Ivy) and Padmashali Kalakriti with Sheridan College. Under the name Rabbit Hole Productions (RBH) we designed this project as a part of our Interactive Narrative II course. Currently, we're building a transmedia narrative project that challenges redemption through the cycle of life and death.


Our Project is now live!





Our story.

As a team we were inspired by the greek mythology, Allegory of the Cave. It's a story that discusses the nature of human perception and how this affect the way we percieve our life. Our main goal was to introduce a modern take on this story in the form of a transmedia narrative that our users can participate in. We began our process by breaking down our chosen story and converting them into narratives that the current generation could easily relate / understand. This narrative incorporates digital platforms and tools such as AR, Instagram, Discord, Twine, Webtoons, etc. for a heightened interactive experience. They are also provided with digital artifacts that they can interact with that help illustrate our narrative. In its core, our narrative is about redemption, and tries to question our own morals and self values through choices that the user makes. We hope you enjoy this experience. Thank you!


This narrative includes material that would be sensitive to certain audiences. User discretion is advised.
